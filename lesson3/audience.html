Target Audience Personas

  1. Local Resident

    This persona has a permanent address within the city limits. They come to the site looking for family programs and businesses they wish to visit for an extended period of time.

    Motivation:
    This persona is looking for local businesses to support and ways to be involved in the community.

  2. Student

    This persona is in Rexburg attending BYUI as a full time student. They are generally single students looking to find bargans and other ways to save money.

    Motivation:
    This persona is looking for ways to save money and cheap entertainment.

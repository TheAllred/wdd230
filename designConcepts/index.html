Adidas: Color

Adidas used color theory very well when the color of the girls pants in the image was the same as the color of the sale notification bar.

Amazon: Consistency

Amazon applied consistency very well in their design by having all their products neatly lined up along the browser page. This helps them sell products because the neat lining of their products reminds the user of a store shelf where they would by things off of,

Google: Visual Alignment

The visual alignment of Google is incredibly effective as it points all of the users attention to the search bar in the center. Everything else is minimized as this is the only thing google wants the user to see first.
